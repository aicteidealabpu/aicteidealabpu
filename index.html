About AICTE IDEA Lab

The AICTE IDEA (Idea, Development, Evaluation & Application) Lab at Panjab University, Chandigarh, is established to promote hands-on learning, innovation and product visualization by encouraging the application of STEM fundamentals. The lab serves as a bridge between academia and industry, fostering real-world problem solving and multidisciplinary research.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To enable a collaborative environment where industry partners with academia for pioneering and impactful technological solutions.

Mission

  • Address industrial problems of the northern region with tailored solutions.
  • Provide hands-on opportunities to students, faculty and staff.
  • Enhance technological competencies aligned with real-world needs.
  • Contribute to nation-building and societal advancement.

What We Do

Student Development

Projects, internships, hackathons and innovation challenges enhancing employability and skills.

Faculty Development

FDPs, industry-linked research and consultancy to strengthen teaching and research quality.

Industry Collaboration

Solving real industrial problems through collaboration with MSMEs and startups.

Facilities & Equipment

Engineering & Electronics

3D Printers, Oscilloscopes, ARM Microcontrollers, ESP32/STM32 boards, LabVIEW, DAQ systems and workstations.

Mechanical & Prototyping

Laser cutter, welding machine, drilling machines, grinders, power tools, precision measuring instruments.
